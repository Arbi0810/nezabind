About Us

They say breakfast is the most important meal of the day, and Bread & Breakfast stands by that. Inspired by an undying love for food, B&B is dedicated to serving the community with delicious and savory breakfast burritos. 

Balancing comfort food with innovative flavors, B&B is nestled in the heart of Los Angeles and was established with you in mind. We want your breakfast to be more than just a meal, so with the utmost fresh and quality ingredients, alongside egg-celent customer service, Bread and Breakfast is bringing you the ultimate breakfast burrito experience.
